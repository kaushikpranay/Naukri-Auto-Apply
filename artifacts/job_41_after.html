Welcome to Naukri.com Security Center

We value the trust you place on Naukri.com and are committed to making your job search a safe and fraud-free experience on our site. However, in the wake of an increasing number of instances of fake job offers, we suggest you to be cautious of fraudulent emails and calls.

6 signs to identify fake job offers:
  1. Asking for money (mostly refundable).
  2. Looking for confidential information ( Credit Card / OTP / Aadhar / PAN, etc).
  3. Badly written job ads (incorrect grammar).
  4. Offering easy hiring criteria (on the spot offer, no interview).
  5. Luring with salary beyond expectation.
  6. Using the names of known job portals (fake domains like naukriservices004@naukrioutlook.com).
4 ways to protect yourself against job frauds:
  1. Never respond without verifying the legitimacy of the job offer.
  2. Do not share personal/bank account details with anyone.
  3. Refuse to make any payment for job offers.
  4. Verify online presence and reviews of the recruitment firm.

If you've received (or receive) any such suspicious email communication, reach out to us via email at compliance@naukri.com

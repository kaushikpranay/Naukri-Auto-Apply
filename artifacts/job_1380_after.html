About the Role

Company: Cabirx Job Type: Full-Time (Probationary to Permanent) Experience Level: Entry-Level (Targeting 2025 & 2026 Graduates) Department: Emerging Technologies & AI Consulting At Cabirx, we are not just another IT consulting firm; we are architecting the future of enterprise technology. Operating across India and expanding rapidly into the Dubai and broader Middle East (GCC) markets, we specialize in deploying next-generation tech solutions—specifically Generative AI, intelligent automation, and financial technology implementations. We operate on a pure meritocracy. We move fast, we build scalable solutions, and we cut out the corporate red tape.

Key Responsibilities

Advanced Prompt Engineering: Design, test, iterate, and refine complex prompts for LLMs (GPT-4, Claude, Llama) to generate highly accurate, business-specific outputs.

AI Workflow Automation: Build and deploy intelligent agents and automated pipelines using frameworks like LangChain, LlamaIndex, or AutoGen.

Quality Assurance & "Red Teaming": Rigorously test AI models for hallucinations, biases, and edge cases. You will act as the logic gatekeeper for AI outputs.

API Integration: Write clean, efficient Python scripts to connect LLM APIs to internal databases, client dashboards, and third-party platforms.

Continuous Innovation: Monitor daily advancements in the Generative AI space, rapid-prototype new use cases, and present your findings to our senior consulting team.

What We're Looking For

Education: Currently in your final year (2025/2026 batch) or a recent graduate with a degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Data Science, or a related logic-heavy field.

The AI Baseline: You have hands-on, personal experience tinkering with LLMs. If your only use of ChatGPT is asking it to write a cover letter, this role is not for you. We want to see how you’ve tried to break, bypass, or build with these models.

Core Technical Skills: Strong foundational programming skills in Python. You must be comfortable writing scripts, managing JSON data, and interacting with REST APIs.

Linguistic Logic: Prompt engineering is essentially programming in plain English. You must possess exceptional written communication skills and a deep understanding of context and nuance.

Problem-Solving: A proven ability to break down complex, ambiguous business problems into step-by-step logical instructions.

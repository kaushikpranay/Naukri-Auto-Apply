About Us
We’re building cutting-edge solutions in the ad-tech space, leveraging AI to deliver scalable, high-performance systems that drive measurable results. About the Role
As a Backend Engineer, you will play a critical role in designing and building the core systems that power our platform. You’ll work closely with product managers, designers, and other engineers to develop scalable backend services, implement efficient APIs, and contribute to the continuous improvement of our systems. This is an individual contributor role with high ownership, where your work will directly influence product functionality, performance, and customer experience.

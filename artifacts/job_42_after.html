Job description

Roles and Responsibilities :

  • Design, develop, test, deploy and maintain large-scale data pipelines using Agilent AI tools.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify business requirements and design solutions that meet those needs.
  • Develop generative models using Generative Artificial Intelligence (AGENTIC) techniques to generate new data insights from existing datasets.
  • Troubleshoot complex issues related to data quality, integrity, and performance in real-time.

Job Requirements :

  • 6-10 years of experience in Data Engineering with expertise in Agilent AI technologies.
  • Strong understanding of Artificial Intelligence (AIML), Generative AI, and Generative Artificial Intelligence (AGENTIC).
  • Experience working on big-data processing frameworks such as Spark or Hadoop.

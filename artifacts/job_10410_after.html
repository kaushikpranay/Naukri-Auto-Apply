Job Description:

We are seeking a qualified and detail-oriented Chartered Accountant (CA) to manage our financial operations, ensure compliance with statutory regulations, and provide strategic insights for financial planning. The ideal candidate will have strong analytical skills, a deep understanding of financial reporting standards, and proven experience in accounting and auditing.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Financial Reporting & Analysis:
    • Prepare accurate monthly, quarterly, and annual financial statements.
    • Conduct variance analysis and provide insights into financial performance.
  • Taxation & Compliance:
    • Ensure compliance with direct and indirect tax laws (e.g., GST, TDS, Income Tax).
    • File tax returns and support audits by government authorities.
  • Auditing & Internal Controls:
    • Conduct internal audits and implement financial control systems.
    • Liaise with external auditors and regulatory bodies.
  • Budgeting & Forecasting:
    • Develop annual budgets and financial forecasts.
    • Monitor budget adherence and recommend corrective actions.
  • Financial Strategy & Advisory:
    • Assist in strategic financial planning, risk management, and investment analysis.
    • Advise management on cost-reduction and profit-maximization strategies.

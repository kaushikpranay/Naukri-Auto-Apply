Software Engineer - Artificial intelligence (AI)

We are seeking an experienced and innovative AI Engineer to join our development team in building advanced AI capabilities for our Salesforce-based cloud product. This product caters to small and medium-sized clinical practices in the US. The ideal candidate will have expertise in both Predictive Machine Learning (ML) and Generative AI, with a strong focus on healthcare and Salesforce integrations.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and implement AI-based features to enhance product capabilities, including both predictive analytics (ML) and generative functionalities.
  • Develop and optimize AI models for key healthcare use cases, such as clinical decision support, patient engagement, and operational efficiency.
  • Integrate AI features with Salesforce, ensuring seamless functionality within the existing platform.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand business requirements, define AI-driven solutions, and deliver high-quality implementations.
  • Ensure compliance with healthcare regulations (e.g., HIPAA) and best practices for data privacy and security.
  • Evaluate and recommend AI tools, frameworks, and technologies to support the product’s vision.
  • Stay updated with advancements in AI/ML, particularly in healthcare, and apply relevant innovations to the product.

Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, or a related field.
  • 3+ years of experience in AI/ML development, including hands-on experience with generative AI models.
  • Proficiency in Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, or similar frameworks.
  • Experience with Salesforce development and APIs.
  • Knowledge of cloud-based AI solutions (e.g., AWS, Google Cloud, Azure).
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and ability to work in an agile environment.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Familiarity of healthcare domain preferred.
  • Experience with natural language processing (NLP) and voice assistant technologies.
  • Previous experience in integrating AI into Salesforce Cloud.

Soft Skills

  • Strong communication and collaboration abilities.
  • Analytical mindset with a focus on delivering business value through AI solutions.
  • Self-motivated and eager to learn emerging technologies.

Why Join Us?

  • Opportunity to work on a cutting-edge AI projects with real-world impact.
  • Collaborative and supportive team environment.
